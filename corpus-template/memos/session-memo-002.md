# The Heart of Gold and Infinite Improbability

This session followed the incident aboard the Heart of Gold. Recorded while the events
were still fresh, as several of them have a tendency to become implausible in retrospect.

## The Ship

The Heart of Gold is the first spacecraft to be powered by the Infinite Improbability
Drive, which was constructed in secret at the Magrathea shipyards and then stolen by
Zaphod Beeblebrox at its own launch ceremony. The drive works by passing through every
conceivable point in every conceivable universe simultaneously. The practical result is
that improbable things happen with alarming frequency in its vicinity.

During our time on the ship I was briefly transformed into a penguin, the tea machine
produced something that was almost, but not quite, entirely unlike tea, and Ford
developed a temporary second head. None of these have had lasting effects, as far as
I can tell.

## Zaphod Beeblebrox

Zaphod is Ford's semi-cousin and the former President of the Imperial Galactic
Government, a role he held on the understanding that the President's job is not to
wield power but to draw attention away from it. He has two heads and three arms, the
extra head having been added at some point for reasons he is evasive about.

I did not like him immediately. I have since revised this to: I do not like him, but I
understand him better than I did. He is performing confidence at a scale that suggests
there is something underneath it he would prefer not to examine.

Key working note: Zaphod's plans, when he has them, are usually better than they look.
The problem is that he usually doesn't have them.

## On Improbability as a Planning Framework

The AI and I discussed whether it is possible to make useful plans when your primary
mode of transport operates by generating improbability. The conclusion we reached was
that it is not possible in the conventional sense, but that certain practices help:

1. Maintain a clear sense of what you actually want, independent of what seems achievable.
   Improbability can deliver things you didn't know to ask for.
2. Don't overfit to current circumstances. The situation is probably about to change.
3. The towel remains essential. This is not a metaphor.
